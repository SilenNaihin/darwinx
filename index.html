Simulation Software

Utilizing a molecular dynamics simulation, we are able to model any environment.

Evolutionary Algorithm

We use an evolutionary algorithm to test and iterate nanorobots in the simulation, according to criteria set by the researcher.

Intuitive UI

Our platform allows nanobot designers to easily enter their specifications, and observe the nanobot's actions in the simulation.

Manufacturing

Once researchers are happy with their nanobot design, we manufacture them using nano-assemblers, and deliver the nanorobots right to their door.
